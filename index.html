About Us

[ENG]
Hi! @Indoweekly is platform to write about world issues,specifically regarding human rights, the environment,politics, and economics.
We chose to write in both Indonesian and English because we wanted everyone to be able to understand the issues that we wanna bring to light.
So, through this, we want to contribute something positive to the world by informing people and initiating change

Don't be shy to send us issues or news you want us to post about! Any input or criticism will be appreciated
